# 🏎️ The "Drive-and-Turn" Shortcut (Moment Direction)

### **The Protocol**
1.  **Pivot Start:** Place your pen on the **Pivot Point**.
2.  **Perpendicular Path:** Move your pen along the **shortest path** to hit the **Line of Action** of the force.
3.  **The Turn:** Once you hit the line, "turn the steering wheel" and drive in the **direction of the force arrow**.
4.  **The Result:** Identify if your turn was **Clockwise (CW)** or **Anti-Clockwise (ACW)**.

---

### **Sign Convention (Standard)**
*   $\circlearrowleft$ **Anti-Clockwise (ACW):** Positive ($+$)
*   $\circlearrowright$ **Clockwise (CW):** Negative ($-$)

---

### **🗒️ Notepad Summary: The Moment Moat**
*   **Varignon's Theorem:** Sum of moments of components = Moment of Resultant.
*   **Perpendicularity is Key:** The "shortest way" is always at $90^\circ$ to the line of action.
*   **Dada Logic:** Don't overthink "which side" to push from. You always start from the center (Pivot) and let the **Force Arrow** dictate the turn.
